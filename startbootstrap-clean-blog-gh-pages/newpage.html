My Favorite Stories

  1. Bloody Nose

    2. One night my brother and I were watching a movie and all of the sudden he stands up and runs to the kitchen asking for a tissue. I scramble up from my seat and see him running with his hand right below his nose. He was dripping everywhere. After turning on the lights you could see it all over his clothes, his hands, his face, it was everywhere!

    • Laugh during the difficulty
    • Love enough to take care
    • Allow for a change in plans

  2. Great Grandpa

    3. My great grandpa passed away this semester. It was sad. Really sad, but something I love with things like this is that you begin to see how much God truely is in your life. I can't tell you how many people kept checking in on me, giving me hugs, asking if I needed anything, etc. People I didn't even expect. Strangers. Even though I was and am sad about his passing I am so blessed to see so many people's love and support.

    • There is a life after this
    • People are kinder than you think

  3. Flying a Kite

    4. There was one day where my friend sporadically bought a kite. We flew it that afternoon. There wasn't much wind, but we had fun running it and getting it high in the air. I'm sure a lot of other people living in the apartment next to where we were flying that kite had fun watching too. It was a moment of pure joy for me. Something so simple can bring the most fun, and I love that.

    • Do simple things
    • Be willing to participate spontaneously
    • Connect with others
